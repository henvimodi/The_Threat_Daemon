Welcome To Threat Daemon
Threat Daemon is a unified threat modeling tool for managing your application design architecture, documentation and security management on a unite platform. It supports various Threat Modeling methodologies such as STRIDE, Attack Tree, DREAD, PASTA and CVSS.
We believed in a unique and effective unified approach.
By using Threat Daemon you can :
  • Generate a threat model within minutes
  • Manage Threat, Vulnerabilities, Risk and its countermeasures on an single platform.
  • Reduce overall risk by remediating identified threats.
  • Create a culture of collaboration between security and development teams.
  • Save cost, time and development rework.
